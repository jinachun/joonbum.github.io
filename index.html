About me

I am a Research Scientist at Battelle. I have been working in a driving safety domain focusing on human factors issues. My research interests include driver distraction, visual attention allocation, and individual differences in driving behaviors.

I love to play soccer. Currently, I play in a Seattle-based amateure league Division 2.
